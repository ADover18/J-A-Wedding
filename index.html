Location

Travelling by Rail

Edinburgh Waverly Station is next door to the Balmoral hotel. When leaving the station ensure you exit at the Princes street exit. On exiting the station turn right. The Balmoral is just a few steps down the road.

Travelling by Plane

Edinburgh Airport is located west of the city. The tram and buses 100 Airlink or X18 take you within a 5 minute walk of the Balmoral. Get off at Princes street and continue to walk down this road. The Balmoral Hotel is last building on the right hand side.

Hotels

If you are looking for a hotel in the city center, there are many within walking distance of the Balmoral. Cheaper options include:

  • Travelodge: Edinburgh Central Waterloo Place
  • Travelodge: Edinburgh Central Princes Street
  • Travelodge: Edinburgh Central
  • Premier Inn: Edinburgh City Centre Royal Mile hotel
  • Premier Inn: Edinburgh City Centre York Place St James Quarter

There are also plenty of mid-range to high-end options within walking distance from the Balmoral. The Edinburgh Festival starts around the beginning of August so hotels do get booked up quite quickly around this time.
